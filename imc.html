What is the importance of healthy eating?

Healthy eating is essential for the proper functioning of the body and mind. By choosing nutritious foods such as fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains, we help prevent diseases such as diabetes and obesity, as well as strengthen the immune system and improve digestion. A balanced diet also contributes to weight management, increases energy, and improves mood. In addition, it positively impacts mental health, reducing stress and anxiety. In summary, a healthy diet is the basis for a balanced life, with more disposition and quality of life.
